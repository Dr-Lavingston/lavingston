Harambee for Peter Otewa’s Heirs

This page was created to support Lavingston Ochieng Peter (Student in MMUST) in raising school fees, alongside Joylet Atieno (St. Gabriel Gwassi Girls) and Mike Rooney (Kisii University).

The Harambee will take place from Wednesday, 5th November to Thursday, 20th November 2025. We are honoured to have Hon. Leonard Ogutu as our Guest of Honour.

Thank you all for joining and for your continued support. Asanteni sana kwa moyo wa harambee!
